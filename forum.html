IoT Technologies and Hardware for Engineering
IoT Systems Engineering

IoT Hardware includes a wide range of devices such as devices for routing, bridges, sensors etc. These IoT devices manage key tasks and functions such as system activation, security, action specifications, communication, and detection of support-specific goals and actions. IoT Hardware components can vary from low-power boards; single-board processors like the Arduino Uno which are basically smaller boards that are plugged into mainboards to improve and increase its functionality by bringing out specific functions or features (such as GPS, light and heat sensors, or interactive displays). A programmer specifies a board's input and output, then creates a circuit design to illustrate the interaction of these inputs and outputs.

FAQ

How do I start a course?

You can start a course 2 ways. First go to our courses page and choose the course you'd like to follow, and at the bottom of every course you'll find a 'Enroll Now' button. Click on it. It'll take you to a page with more details of the course and at the end of the page, you'll see a payment option. Complete the payment and one of our representatives will contact you within 24 hours. Otherwise, you can call our call center to get a quotation for your needs.

Can I get a refund?

It is possible to get a refund within two weeks after starting your course. So, contact our representatives before it's late. If you still want to get a refund after two weeks, you may contact our representatives to see if that's possible.

Can I keep all the recordings of the course?

Yes. You can copy our recordings folder provided to your OneDrive, or Google Drive and access them after anytime you want. It's yours for the lifetime!

When does the course start?

Different courses have different timeframes, so you either need to email or call us to get the time.
