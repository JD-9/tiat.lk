About Us

Our mission is to increase the employment of software engineers in Sri Lanka by teaching the students exaclty how this whole thing works, and how it looks like in and out. It is not a simple task to become a software engineer and get hired. That's where we come in to help any student in need of almost everything related to coding.

Who We Are

We are a group of software engineers, designers, software architects, and enthusiast programmers teamed up together to share our experiences with those students who'd want to become software engineers, architects, and designers. Universities are great, but what you do as a software engineer, or an architect is different from what you learn at a university. We're here to help you with that to become the guy you've always wanted to be.

Get to Know More About Us

You can get to know more about us by visiting our social media pages, like YouTube and Instagram. From there you'll be able to see how our teachers teach. Also, you may write to us using our email or just message us on Twitter or Facebook, and we'll contact you back.
